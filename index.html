Welcome to Learning to Read Classical Tibetan

Bemused by the loss of the Tibetan for Language Students website this little site has been set up as a place to collate materials for learning to read Classical Tibetan.

At present the site is under construction, but it's hoped with time there will be a simple Extended Wylie Markdown down editor for creating your own resources (or practice writing) or contributing, a collection of lessons as I learn to read Classical Tibetan myself, and later on an area to test out knowledge or alphabet (in UChen script), vocabulary and even simple translations.

Being a novice I can't make any guarantees that the material on this site is correct. If you notice any errors or omissions than I offer humble apologies.
